The BCS digital IT apprenticeship standards ...

# Analyst
## Data Analyst (level 4)
Data informs every business decision in the digital age, and analysts who can explore, interpret and present this data are in high demand.
## IS Business Analyst (level 4)
Prepare your apprenticeship learners for the multi-faceted and increasingly valuable role of IS business analyst.
# Cyber security
## Cyber Intrusion Analyst (level 4)
Develop the technical skills your apprenticeship learner needs to effectively detect and escalate the cyber security breaches in their organisation.
##Cyber Security Technologist (level 4)
Equip your apprenticeship learner with the skills and competencies to become a valued member of the cyber security team.
# Hardware, network, cloud & infrastructure
## Infrastructure Technician (level 3)
Infrastructure technician apprentices will use your training to support their colleagues and clients in using technology more productively.
## Unified Communications Technician (level 3)
Deliver the competencies apprentices need to install communication hardware and software and support an organisation’s comms systems.
## IT Solutions Technician - Hardware / Software (level 3)
Provide apprentices with the skills to develop, implement and maintain complete IT solutions such as networks, operating systems and applications.
## Unified Communications Trouble Shooter (level 4)
Your training will enable apprentices to install equipment and software, and handle faults and requests for help across unified comms technologies.
## Network Engineer (level 4)
Provide your apprenticeship learners with the skills to design, install, configure and maintain the communication networks in their organisation.
#Software development
## Software Development Technician (level 3)
Provide apprentices with the essential skills toolkit for success as part of their organisation’s software development team.
## Software Developer (level 4)
Turn your level 4 apprenticeship learners into proficient developers capable of overseeing straightforward elements of the software development project.
## Software Tester (level 4)
Software testers are key to every IT development project, ensuring software is fit for purpose - equip your level 4 apprentices with these valuable skills.
# Digital marketing
## Digital Marketer (level 3)
Deliver the learning for this popular apprenticeship standard and support employers in driving customer engagement and growing their business.
# IT sales
## IT Technical Salesperson (level 3)
Provide your apprenticeship learners with highly-demanded and proven selling techniques and expertise, shaped to the specific needs of their business.
