---
title:  SI prefixes
---

Many SI prefixes
are used in an unhelpful way.

 - Example: three years ago Facebook was generating **4 petabytes of new data per day**.
 - Even if you don't need to visit a
[chart of SI, or metric, prefixes](https://www.nist.gov/pml/weights-and-measures/metric-si-prefixes)
4 peta- things is not very helpful. If I want one hundredth of that value
I have to know the next smallest prefix. 
 - How about 
**4&times;10<sup>15</sup> bytes of new data per day**, or 
for those limited by the character set there is
**4e15 bytes of new data per day**.
