Units
=====

All numeric measurements in text to be qualified where necessary.
Examples:

- the budget is 5 billion dollars (5e9 USD)
- three years ago Facebook was generating 4 petabytes (4e15 bytes) of new data per day
- three years ago Facebook was generating 4 pebibytes (4&times;(1024)<sup>5</sup> bytes) of new data per day

Notes:

- Use of IEC binary prefixes will only appear in quoted material.
- Use of SI prefixes which are not integer powers of 1000 will only appear in quoted material.
- One bit is one binary digit.
- One bytes is 8 bits.
- One bite is a mouthful.
