Another day, another slogan.

It has been 13 days since the last post, so here is an update to celebrate
the new directive from the UK Government. I think it would be fair to say
that there have been some rather unclear messages coming from Downing
Street, and in retrospect their Communications approach could have been
better.

I have found the BBC an invaluable resource recently. I was somewhat reluctant to
pay the license fee last month, but some of their programmes are
hilarious, the best being BBC News and BBC Breakfast. There are also some Comedy programmes if you
can find the older stuff such as Dad's Army (1968 - 1977),
The League of Gentleman (1999 - 2002),
Not The Nine O'Clock News (1979 - 1982), etc.
The Conservatives complain about the left-wing bias of the BBC, and Labour of a right-wing bias, but the only
real bias is that dictated by common sense: don't bite the hand that feeds you.
